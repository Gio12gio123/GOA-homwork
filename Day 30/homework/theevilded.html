Evil Dead Rise

"Evil Dead Rise" is a horror film that serves as a continuation of the iconic "Evil Dead" franchise. Released in 2023, the movie is directed by Lee Cronin and produced by Sam Raimi, Bruce Campbell, and Robert Tapert, who were all involved in the original series.

The plot centers on two estranged sisters whose reunion is cut short by the rise of flesh-possessing demons, thrusting them into a primal battle for survival. Unlike its predecessors, which were set in isolated woodland cabins, "Evil Dead Rise" takes place in an urban environment, adding a fresh and terrifying twist to the franchise's traditional setting.

The film is praised for its intense atmosphere, gruesome special effects, and the relentless horror that fans of the series have come to expect.
