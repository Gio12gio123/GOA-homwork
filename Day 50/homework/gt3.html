Porsche 911 GT3 RS

Specifications

Engine: 4.0L flat-six

Power: 518 hp

0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds

Top Speed: 196 mph

Weight: 1,430 kg (3,150 lbs)

Transmission: 7-speed PDK automatic

Overview

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a high-performance sports car that embodies the spirit of racing. With its lightweight construction, aerodynamic design, and powerful engine, the GT3 RS delivers an exhilarating driving experience both on the road and on the track.
